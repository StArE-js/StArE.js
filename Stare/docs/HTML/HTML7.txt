<p>The advanced master “Helicopter, Aircraft and Drone Architecture” provides high-level engineering and technical skills for careers in the aircraft, Drone &amp; helicopter business.</p>
<p>The programme covers technical &amp; management aspects related to design,<br class='autobr' />
certification and operations of civil/military aircrafts, drones &amp; helicopters.</p>
<p>The advanced master “Helicopter, Aircraft and Drone Architecture” leads to several positions in design, certification and operations of civil and military aircrafts, drones and helicopters in France and overseas.</p>
