the park[edit]
taza national park was established in 1923 by the french colonial administrators in algeria. its aim was to protect the coastline and towering forested heights and provide protection for the barbary macaque.&#91;3&#93; the park adjoins the mediterranean sea, and includes cliffs that rise from sea level to over 1,100&#160;m (3,600&#160;ft).
