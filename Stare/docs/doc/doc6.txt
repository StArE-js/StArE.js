hada awards $375,000 in scholarships to houston students
three spring area students receive $3,000 each towards college tuition.
houston (august 5, 2015) &#8211; the houston automobile dealers association (hada) issued $375,000 in scholarships to 125 deserving college students this week, providing financial support to houston area dealership employees, their dependents and ayes students.
three students from the spring area received $3,000 scholarships to attend college. nathan murphy attending yale and reynaldo cienfuegos attending the university of houston-downtown, were selected from fred haas toyota scion world. halie fretwell was selected from the automotive youth program at carl wunsche senior high school and will attend san jacinto college.
